Easy Street Tacos

Description

These are quick, authentic street-style tacos made with seasoned beef, fresh cilantro, and onions on warm corn tortillas.

Ingredients

  • Flank steak or ground beef
  • Corn tortillas
  • White onion, diced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Cook and season your meat in a hot skillet until browned.
  2. Warm the corn tortillas on a separate pan or griddle.
  3. Assemble the tacos by layering meat, onions, and cilantro on the tortillas.
  4. Serve warm with a squeeze of fresh lime juice.
