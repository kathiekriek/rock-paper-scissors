Fresh Caesar Salad

Description

A crisp, refreshing classic Caesar salad featuring crunchy romaine lettuce, garlic croutons, and creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Wash, dry, and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place lettuce in a large bowl and toss with the Caesar dressing.
  3. Top with a generous handful of croutons and shredded parmesan cheese.
